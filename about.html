About

Fut||r[t]istic||Hub is a community of people interested in technology, art and how technology can help rethink and renew art, hopefully reaching new languages and new ways of expression.

We aim to provide a point of aggregation for anyone interested in how the idea we have of the future can materialize in new concepts of art. Act as a hub where different interests can meet and cooperate.

A sort of interdisciplinary Agora for photographers, painters, programmers, art-lovers, mathematicians, future-lovers, engineers, technology-lovers, where artists will be interested in sharing their ideas, their artefacts and where ultra-technologists can share their code and their algorithms for generative art.

Tech-Art-Lovers will find here the right hub where their two passions, technology and art marry and become a new form of expression. They will find information about exhibitions, workshops and collections, when, where and how.
